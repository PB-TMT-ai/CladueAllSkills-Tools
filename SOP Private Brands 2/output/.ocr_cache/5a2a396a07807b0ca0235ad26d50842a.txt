SOP for Influencer Meeting: Mason, Contractor, Architects & Engineers
Counter Selection (at month beginning)
Curated list of dealer counters is shared in an excel sheet with the Sales team by HO basis
defined business objectives:
- Stock liquidation
- Prospect dealer counter conversion
- Build performing dealer confidence
Every Sales Team member selects 4-5 dealer counters for meet planning and share
tentative meeting dates on the same excel sheet.
Influencer Selection
Pre Meet:
• All the influencers set to attend the meeting must:
- Be listed in the Influencer master sheet
- Have a valid Sf ID (Name, Profession, Mobile number, Pin code)
- Be tagged to a particular dealer
• The final attendee list for a meeting must be shared with Head office at least
one day prior to the meeting.
• ADHOC replacements of influencers to meets is only permissible for Mason
meets given their data is shared with Head office & their Sf IDs created within
two days post the meeting. (Max ADHOC replacements = 25% of the total
attendees)
Post Meet:
• Against every influencer invited to the meeting, the sales team to update the
following:
- Whether the influencer attended the meeting (Yes or No)
Meet Reimbursement
Reimbursement will be made only for those influencers who are part of the attendee list
shared prior to the meeting with ADHOC provisions kept only for mason meets.
